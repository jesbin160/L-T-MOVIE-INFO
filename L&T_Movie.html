The Shawshank Redemption

Drama | 1994

Synopsis

Andy Dufresne, a successful banker, is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for the murder of his wife and her lover, despite his claims of innocence. Over the years, he befriends Ellis "Red" Redding, a lifer who knows how to get things inside prison.

Through acts of decency, persistence, and hope, Andy transforms the lives of those around him and ultimately finds a way to reclaim his freedom in the most unexpected way.
